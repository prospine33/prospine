Join the Future of Non-Surgical Spinal Care with Pro Spine

At Pro Spine, we are revolutionizing spinal rehabilitation through cutting-edge non-invasive therapies. As pioneers in Non-Invasive Robotic Spinal Decompression Therapy, Advanced Manual Therapy Techniques, and Electrotherapy, we offer a dynamic platform for professionals passionate about physiotherapy, ergonomics, and spinal care.

Why Choose a Career at Pro Spine?

1. Innovative Work Environment

Access the latest technology including Spine 3D™ Analysis, Robotic Spinal Decompression, Shock Wave Therapy, and Dual Frequency Ultrasound Therapy.

2. Professional Growth & Development

Continuous learning through Pro Spine Academy with training in advanced techniques like Maitland Mobilization, McKenzie Method, and more.

3. Pro Spine Academy

A premier training institute led by Dr. Pranav Kumar, offering hands-on workshops and certifications in spinal rehabilitation and ergonomics.

4. Work with the Best

Join a team led by Dr. Pranav Kumar, a renowned Physical Therapist and Ergonomist with over 20 years of experience.

5. Patient-Centric Approach

Focus on non-surgical, pain-free spinal care combining technology and manual therapy for optimal patient recovery.

6. Expanding Opportunities

Grow with Pro Spine Bangalore and explore diverse roles in physiotherapy, research, and healthcare management.

Career Paths at Pro Spine

  • Physiotherapists & Rehabilitation Specialists – Work with cutting-edge spinal care technology.
  • Ergonomists & Workplace Wellness Consultants – Prevent and manage work-related musculoskeletal issues.
  • Clinical Trainers & Educators – Contribute to skill development through Pro Spine Academy.
  • Healthcare Administrators – Manage operations and patient care services.
  • Research & Development Professionals – Innovate non-surgical spinal care techniques.
